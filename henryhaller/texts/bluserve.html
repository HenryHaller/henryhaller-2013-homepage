Bluserve;

Bluserve is a project to bring quality bluetooth music playback to through linux devices. This is already possible with Bluez and pulseaudio, but Bluserve aims to simplify the process and make it easy for any end-user to control via their smartphone. I've managed to get it running on a raspberry pi!. The smartphone app is also working, and in alpha/beta stage. Check back here for more updates! Please feel free to browse the source on Github.
