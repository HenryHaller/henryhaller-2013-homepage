Bitcoin;

If you're not familiar with what bitcoin is, I'm slightly jealous because right now you get to discover something new and exciting! In a nutshell, bitcoin is internet-style money. Jargon alert: It's a fully decentralized/distributed peer-to-peer digital currency that is impossible to counterfeit; its supply is limited, known and asymptotically increasing towards 21 million. If that sounds interesting to you, I recommend you check out bitcoin.org, Wikipedia and Coinbase. I've been following bitcoin since early 2011 so I can remember when its market cap was under $8 million, there were a handful of tweets about it total and the original client didn't even encrypt wallet files! (Yes I did search for "wallet.dat" in limewire but unfortunately there was no intersection between those foolish enough to index their entire harddrive and those savvy enough to be using bitcoin already.) How far we've come in a few years and I still think it's only gaining more momentum. I've been fascinated with it ever since, I'm also a big geek about cryptography in general though I can't pretend to understand most of the math involved, only the general theories and their practical applications. Bitcoin is a practical application that everyone can take advantage of, so download a client or make a web-wallet account (for example: Coinbase) and begin accepting bitcoin today.
